What you'll get from here

Edu Connect provides following features for Teachers and Students.

For Students

  1. Students can login to the portal and keep themselves updated of all the activities from dashboard.
  2. Students can give their attendance by swapping their ID card and the system automatically records it.
  3. Students can see the notice posted by their teacher in the Class Notice option.
  4. Students can see and download their assignments posted by the teacher in the Assignments option.
  5. They can also submit their assignments. (Once assignment is submitted, it can't be changed later.)
  6. Students can find any teacher from All Teachers List and can also contact them.
  7. Students can check their exam marks in the Marks option.

For Teacher

  1. Teachers can add student in their class through Add Student option.
  2. After adding students in their class, Teacher can record the attendance of students.
  3. Teacher can also give or edit their class students marks.
  4. Teachers can post notice which will be sent to all students in their class.
  5. Teachers can upload assignments through upload assignment options.
  6. Teacher can check their student's assignment submission and give them marks accordingly.
  7. Teacher can also all the marks given by them to a student by accessing their Marks profile and can edit their marks.
